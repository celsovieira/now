Information

The conference will take place in two sessions over four days.
The first sessions on 24-25th March will have a hybrid format.
The second session on 27-28th March will occur remotely.
The presentations in the first two days will take place at the RUB in the room GA 3/ 132
The third and fourth days will occur remotely.

You can join all the presentations online including the first two days via Zoom here

For any inquiries, please send an email to celso.deoliveiravieira@rub.de
